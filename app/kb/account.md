# ProcessNexus Account Management

## Creating an Account
Sign up at processnexus.com. Email verification required.

## Billing
- Free plan: no credit card required
- Standard: billed monthly; cancel any time
- Enterprise: invoiced annually; contract required

## Cancellation
Downgrade or cancel in Account Settings → Billing.
Data is retained for 30 days after cancellation.

## Support
- Free: community forum
- Standard: support@processnexus.com
- Enterprise: dedicated Slack channel
